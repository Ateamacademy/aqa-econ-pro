## Paper 3 — Edexcel B A-Level Economics (9EB0/03)

**Total: 100 marks · Duration: 2 hours · 2 questions**

### Structure
- **Question 1** — 50 marks, with extracts A & B (one prose, one data table)
- **Question 2** — 50 marks, with extracts C & D (one prose, one data table)

### Mark allocation per question (fixed)
| Part | Marks | Command word | Rubric style |
|------|-------|--------------|--------------|
| (a)  | 8     | Explain (with diagram) | Level-based (3 levels) |
| (b)  | 10    | Analyse (data-led) | Level-based (4 levels) |
| (c)  | 12    | Assess | Level-based (4 levels) |
| (d)  | 20    | Evaluate | Level-based (4 levels) |

### Marking
Use the level descriptors and AO splits already defined for Paper 2 in `mem://features/edexcel-b-paper2-pattern.md` (8/10/12/20 mark bands are identical between Papers 2 and 3). All four parts use **level-based** marking; no point-based rubrics on Paper 3 (no 4- or 6-mark parts).

### Validation
- Each paper must contain exactly 2 questions, each with parts (a)–(d).
- Marks per question must sum to 50; total paper = 100.
- Part (a) must require a diagram.
